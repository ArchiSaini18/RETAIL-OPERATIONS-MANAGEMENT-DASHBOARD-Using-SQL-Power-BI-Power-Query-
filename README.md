# RETAIL & OPERATIONS MANAGEMENT DASHBOARD (Using SQL, Power BI & Power Query)

An interactive business intelligence dashboard built using SQL, Power BI, Power Query, and DAX to analyze retail sales performance, product profitability, regional trends, and revenue growth—enabling stakeholders to make data-driven operational and strategic decisions.

📄 **Description**

• This dashboard provides a comprehensive overview of retail operations by analyzing sales transactions, product categories, regional performance, profit margins, and monthly revenue trends.

• SQL was used to extract, transform, and analyze raw retail data, which was further processed using Power Query and visualized through interactive Power BI reports.

• The dashboard enables users to monitor sales performance, identify top-performing products, evaluate regional profitability, and track revenue trajectories over time.

• Interactive filters allow stakeholders to drill down into specific regions and product categories for deeper business insights.

💻 **SQL Analysis & Data Processing**

Structured and executed advanced SQL queries to transform raw retail sales data into meaningful business insights, supporting reporting automation and operational decision-making.

**Key Contributions**:

• Extracted and aggregated retail transaction data to calculate total sales, profit, quantity sold, and profit margins.

• Developed time-series analysis queries to track monthly sales trends and revenue growth patterns.

• Utilized SQL window functions for cumulative revenue tracking and performance trend analysis.

• Analyzed regional sales performance to identify high-revenue and high-profit markets.

• Evaluated product category performance to determine key revenue drivers and profit contributors.

• Built ranking queries to identify top-selling products and support inventory planning.

• Processed and transformed datasets using SQL operations such as:

JOIN
GROUP BY
ORDER BY
CASE WHEN
COUNT()
SUM()
AVG()
RANK()
ROW_NUMBER()
WINDOW FUNCTIONS
DATE FUNCTIONS
